# Workshop Curriculum — Detailed Design | v1.0.0 | 2026-02-03

## Track 1: "Build Your Startup with an AI Co-Founder" (4 Hours)

### Target Audience
- Aspiring entrepreneurs with a business idea
- Non-technical professionals wanting to start a side project
- Career changers moving into entrepreneurship
- Students in business/MBA programs

### Prerequisites
- A laptop with internet access
- A business idea (even rough/early)
- Gmail account (for Google OAuth integrations)

### Hour 1: The AI Co-Founder Revolution (Presentation + Demo)

**Content:**
- The changing landscape: why technical barriers are falling
- What is an AI co-founder? (concept and capabilities)
- Live demo: Building a SaaS MVP with Resto in real-time
  - Start from template
  - Voice conversation to define features
  - Watch code being generated
  - Deploy to production
- Q&A

**Outcome:** Participants understand what's possible and are excited to try.

### Hour 2: Your Business Foundation (Hands-On)

**Content:**
- Sign up for ATR platform
- Browse template gallery — find the closest match to their idea
- Complete guided wizard:
  - Business basics (name, type, industry, problem)
  - Target customer definition
  - Revenue model selection
- Connect accounts (GitHub, Gmail — pre-configured for workshop)

**Outcome:** Each participant has a configured project on ATR.

### Hour 3: Building with Resto (Hands-On)

**Content:**
- Enter conversational deep-dive with Resto
- Resto asks questions about their specific business
- Participants see their business plan generated
- Review checklist
- Start execution: Resto begins building their first feature
- Watch the code generation and deployment in real-time

**Outcome:** Each participant has a started project with at least one feature deployed.

### Hour 4: What's Next (Review + Wrap-Up)

**Content:**
- Review what was built in 3 hours
- Share projects with the group (optional)
- How to continue building after the workshop
- Subscription options and special workshop pricing
- Networking opportunity
- Certificate of completion

**Outcome:** Participants have a clear path forward and a compelling reason to subscribe.

## Track 2: "Transform Your Operations with an AI Tech Leader/Orchestrator" (8 Hours)

### Target Audience
- Business executives and managers at SMBs
- Operations leaders looking to automate
- Department heads wanting to leverage AI
- Consultants looking to offer AI transformation services

### Prerequisites
- A laptop with internet access
- Access to at least one business tool they currently use (CRM, email, etc.)
- An operational challenge they want to solve

### Hours 1-2: AI Strategy Assessment

**Content:**
- The AI transformation opportunity for existing businesses
- Framework: Identify → Prioritize → Implement → Measure
- Group exercise: Map your current business processes
- Identify top 5 AI automation opportunities
- Prioritization matrix: effort vs impact
- Select top 1-2 opportunities for the workshop

**Outcome:** Clear understanding of where AI adds most value in their business.

### Hours 3-4: Connecting Your Business to Resto

**Content:**
- Sign up and configure ATR
- Connect existing business tools:
  - Email (Gmail/Outlook)
  - CRM (if available)
  - Analytics (Google Analytics)
  - Communication (Slack/Discord)
- Configure AI co-founder for operations context
- Define autonomy levels for business operations

**Outcome:** Resto connected to their real business tools.

### Hours 5-6: Building Automations and Internal Tools

**Content:**
- Use Resto to build:
  - Automated reporting dashboard
  - Customer communication templates
  - Internal workflow automation
  - Data analysis and insights
- Voice interaction practice ("Show me this week's metrics")
- Real results from real data

**Outcome:** At least one working automation using their real business data.

### Hours 7-8: Transformation Roadmap

**Content:**
- Review what was built and the impact
- Design a 90-day AI transformation roadmap
- ROI estimation for AI implementations
- Change management considerations
- Team training strategies
- Ongoing support options and enterprise pricing
- Group presentations of roadmaps
- Certificate of completion

**Outcome:** An actionable 90-day plan and confidence to execute.

## Workshop Operations

### Pricing Strategy
- Track 1 (4h): $500-$1,000 per seat
- Track 2 (8h): $1,500-$3,000 per seat
- Early bird discounts: 20% off
- Group rates: 15% off for 3+
- Corporate packages: Custom pricing for 10+ seats

### Class Size
- Minimum: 10 participants (to run)
- Maximum: 30 participants (quality of experience)
- Ideal: 15-20 participants

### Facilitator Requirements
- Deep knowledge of ATR platform
- Business background (can speak to strategy, not just tech)
- Teaching/presentation skills
- Technical support person as co-facilitator

### Materials
- Workshop slides (branded ATR)
- Participant workbook (printed or digital)
- Quick reference cards for Resto commands
- Certificate template
- Follow-up email sequence (post-workshop nurture)
