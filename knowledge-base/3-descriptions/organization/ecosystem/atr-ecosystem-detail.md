# ATR Ecosystem — Detailed Description | v1.0.0 | 2026-02-03

## Overview

The ATR ecosystem is a vertically integrated pipeline designed to discover, validate, fund, and scale AI-powered businesses. At every stage, the AI co-founder (Resto) is the primary tool.

## Revenue Model Across Ecosystem

| Stage | Revenue Source | Type |
|-------|---------------|------|
| Hackathons | Corporate sponsorships, entry fees | Event-based |
| Fellowship | Program fees or equity | Variable |
| Accelerator | Equity stake (typical: 5-7%) | Long-term |
| Resto Platform | Subscription + credits | Recurring |
| Premium Training | Workshop fees (per-seat) | Service |

## Hackathon Stage — Detailed

### Format
- 24-48 hour intensive events
- Mix of in-person and virtual
- Corporate challenges from real companies
- 50+ per year target

### Resto Integration at Hackathon
- Each participant team gets a Resto instance
- First hands-on experience with the AI co-founder
- Resto helps with: ideation, code scaffolding, pitch prep, demo creation
- Creates "aha moment" — participants see what's possible

### Outcomes for Participants
- Cash prizes for winners
- Pilot contracts with corporate sponsors
- Job offers from corporate partners
- Fellowship invitations for promising teams
- A started project they can continue building on ATR

### Conversion Metrics
- Hackathon participant → Resto free trial: target 60%+
- Hackathon winner → Fellowship application: target 80%+

## Fellowship Stage — Detailed

### Format
- 3-month program
- Cohort-based (10-20 teams per cohort)
- Weekly meetups (virtual or in-person)
- Daily access to Resto with enhanced capabilities

### Focus Areas
- **Team formation**: AI-powered cofounder matching
- **Product deepening**: Full spec with Resto, architecture decisions
- **GTM strategy**: ICP definition, messaging, channel strategy
- **First pilots**: Connect with corporate partners for pilot deals
- **Pitch practice**: Investor-ready pitch with Resto coaching

### Resto's Enhanced Role During Fellowship
- Full business context loaded from hackathon
- GTM-focused agent capabilities unlocked
- Market research and competitor analysis
- Financial modeling assistance
- Pitch deck generation and iteration

### Outcomes
- Validated business model
- First paying customer or signed pilot
- Investor-ready pitch
- Accelerator invitation for top performers

## Accelerator Stage — Detailed

### Format
- 3-month intensive program
- Seed funding ($25K-$150K range, TBD)
- Physical co-working space + virtual program
- Intensive mentorship

### Focus Areas
- Seed funding deployment
- Scaling product with Resto managing full operations
- Enterprise customer introductions
- Series A preparation
- Founder skill development

### Resto at Full Power
- Manages daily business operations
- Code generation and deployment automation
- Marketing campaign execution
- Financial tracking and reporting
- Investor update generation

### Outcomes
- Revenue-generating business
- Clear path to Series A
- ATR alumni network access
- Ongoing Resto subscription

## Premium Training Workshops

### Track 1: "Bring Your Idea to Life with an AI Co-Founder" (4 hours)
**Target:** Aspiring entrepreneurs, non-technical founders
**Content:**
- Hour 1: Introduction to AI co-founder concept, live demo
- Hour 2: Template selection, account setup, planning phase
- Hour 3: Hands-on building with Resto (guided)
- Hour 4: Review, next steps, subscription options

### Track 2: "Transform Your Operations with an AI Tech Leader/Orchestrator" (8 hours)
**Target:** Business executives, SMB owners
**Content:**
- Hours 1-2: AI strategy assessment, identifying opportunities
- Hours 3-4: Connecting existing tools and data to Resto
- Hours 5-6: Building internal tools and automations
- Hours 7-8: Roadmap for AI transformation, ongoing support options

### Pricing Philosophy
- Premium pricing (high value, high touch)
- Limited seats per workshop (exclusivity)
- In-person first, then online cohorts
- Certificate of completion
- Includes trial subscription to Resto

### Scaling Path
1. Live in-person workshops (founder-led)
2. Live virtual workshops (expand reach)
3. Recorded + cohort-based (scale)
4. Self-paced with community (maximum scale)
5. Certification program (brand value)
