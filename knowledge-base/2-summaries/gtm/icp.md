# Ideal Customer Profile | v1.0.0 | 2026-02-03

ATR's primary customers are non-technical entrepreneurs who want to build digital businesses.

**Primary ICP:**
- Non-technical founders with business vision and domain expertise
- Ambitious, curious, willing to learn
- Want to build SaaS, e-commerce, or marketplace businesses
- Currently blocked by lack of technical co-founder or development team
- Budget for subscription + premium services

**Secondary ICP:**
- Business executives wanting to leverage AI in existing operations
- Target for "Transform your operations with an AI Tech Leader/Orchestrator" workshops
- Decision-makers at SMBs looking to automate and scale

**Tertiary ICP:**
- Technical founders who want to move faster
- Solo developers who need business strategy support
- Hackathon participants looking to turn projects into businesses

**Where to Find Them:**
- ATR hackathons and fellowship programs (owned channel)
- Startup communities, incubators, co-working spaces
- LinkedIn, X (Twitter), entrepreneurship forums
- Business schools and executive education programs
- Podcast and content marketing around AI entrepreneurship
