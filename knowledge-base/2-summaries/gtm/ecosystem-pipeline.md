# Ecosystem Pipeline | v1.0.0 | 2026-02-03

ATR's ecosystem serves as a discovery and conversion pipeline for the core product (Resto).

**Pipeline Flow:**
Hackathon → Fellowship → Accelerator → Scaled Business (all powered by Resto)

**Hackathon Stage:**
- First touchpoint with Resto
- Participants build projects using AI co-founder
- Corporate challenges provide real-world problems
- Cash prizes, pilots, and job offers as incentives
- Conversion to fellowship: top performers invited

**Fellowship Stage:**
- Deep-dive with team + Resto
- GTM strategy development with AI co-founder
- First pilot customer connections
- Product-market fit validation
- Duration: ~3 months
- Conversion to accelerator: validated teams

**Accelerator Stage:**
- Seed funding and structured support
- Resto manages full business operations
- Fundraising preparation, investor introductions
- Enterprise customer introductions
- Duration: ~3 months

**Post-Accelerator:**
- Ongoing Resto subscription
- Alumni network access
- Growth capital connections
- Target: $100M+ ARR companies
