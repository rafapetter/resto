# Success Metrics & KPIs | v1.0.0 | 2026-02-03

Key metrics to track ATR platform health and growth. Targets set for first 6 months post-launch.

**Adoption:**
- Registered users: 500+ (6mo target)
- Workshop attendees: 200+ (6mo target)
- Hackathon participants using Resto: 60%+

**Activation:**
- Users completing onboarding (planning phase done): 40% of signups
- Users with at least one connected account: 70% of onboarded

**Engagement:**
- Weekly active conversations per user: 5+ sessions/week
- Voice interaction adoption: track % of users using voice

**Value Delivery:**
- Businesses with deployed applications: 50+
- Tasks completed successfully by agent: 85%+ completion rate

**Revenue:**
- MRR from subscriptions + credits: TBD based on pricing
- Training revenue: track per workshop

**Retention:**
- Month-over-month retention: 70%+
- Net promoter score (NPS): 50+

**Agent Quality:**
- Task completion rate: 85%+
- User intervention rate (how often user must correct agent): track and minimize
- Knowledge base auto-update accuracy: track and optimize
