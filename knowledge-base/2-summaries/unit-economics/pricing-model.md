# Pricing Model | v1.0.0 | 2026-02-03

Hybrid subscription + usage with credits/wallet system. Modeled after Cursor and Anthropic pricing patterns.

**Subscription Tiers (Monthly/Annual):**
- Each tier includes base quotas: AI requests, voice minutes, storage, connected accounts
- Higher tiers unlock more quotas, faster models, priority execution, and advanced features
- Specific pricing TBD based on cost modeling

**Credits/Wallet System:**
- When users exceed tier quotas, credits are consumed from their wallet
- Credits are fungible across all platform activities (AI tokens, voice, compute, storage)
- Manual top-up or auto-recharge options
- Spending limits configurable by user
- Clear cost attribution per action in dashboard

**Alerts & Transparency:**
- Notification at 80% quota consumption
- Low wallet balance alerts
- Auto-recharge triggers (user-configurable)
- Real-time usage dashboard
- Cost breakdown by category

**Revenue Streams:**
1. Subscriptions + credits (core product) — Recurring, high margin
2. Premium training workshops — Service, very high margin
3. Hackathon sponsorships — Event-based, medium margin
4. Accelerator equity/fees — Long-term variable upside
