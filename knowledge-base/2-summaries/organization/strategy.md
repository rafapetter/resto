# ATR Strategy | v1.0.0 | 2026-02-03

**Core Product:** Resto — a cloud-based AI co-founder agent. This is the centerpiece of ATR. Everything else feeds into it.

**Beachhead Market:** Non-technical entrepreneurs building SaaS tools (priority 1), E-commerce businesses (priority 2), and Marketplaces (priority 3).

**Go-to-Market Approach:** Product-led growth supported by ecosystem events and premium services.

**Ecosystem Strategy:**
- Hackathons: Discover talent, first hands-on experience with Resto
- Fellowship: Deep-dive — team building, GTM strategy, first pilot connections
- Accelerator: Fund and scale with Resto as the primary building tool

**Premium Services:**
- 4-hour intensive workshops: "Build your startup with an AI co-founder"
- 8-hour full-day workshops: "Transform your operations with an AI Tech Leader/Orchestrator"
- Target: Non-technical entrepreneurs and business executives
- Premium pricing

**Revenue Streams:** Subscriptions + usage credits (core), premium training (services), hackathon sponsorships (events), accelerator equity (long-term).
