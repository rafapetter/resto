# ATR Ecosystem | v1.0.0 | 2026-02-03

The ATR ecosystem is a pipeline designed to discover, validate, and scale AI-powered businesses. Resto (the AI co-founder) is introduced at every stage.

**Phase 1 — Hackathons (Discover):**
- 50+ hackathons/year with corporate challenges
- First hands-on experience with Resto as co-founder
- Participants build hackathon projects WITH their AI co-founder
- Winners get fellowship invitations

**Phase 2 — Fellowship (Validate):**
- Deep-dive with team + AI co-founder
- Focus on GTM strategy and first pilot connections
- AI co-founder helps with market research, pitch prep, product specs
- Duration: ~3 months

**Phase 3 — Accelerator (Scale):**
- Seed funding and structured support
- Resto runs full business operations
- Fundraising prep, scaling, enterprise sales
- Duration: ~3 months

**Phase 4 — Scale:**
- Ongoing Resto subscription for business operations
- Access to ATR alumni network, enterprise customers, growth capital
- Goal: $100M+ ARR for portfolio companies

**Flywheel:** Hackathons → Fellowship → Accelerator → Scaled Business (all powered by Resto)
